Solar-Powered System

Completely off-grid operation with solar panels powering Raspberry Pi 4 cameras. No electrical infrastructure needed for remote farm locations.

On-Device AI Detection

Local AI model on Raspberry Pi 4 detects fire, smoke, and intrusions in real-time. Immediate response without internet dependency.

Dual AI Verification

Detected frames sent to our powerful AI server for re-analysis and confirmation, ensuring 95%+ accuracy with minimal false alarms.

Real-Time Alerts

Instant notifications with detection images sent simultaneously to supervisor web dashboard and farmer mobile app.

Smart Irrigation

Automated irrigation system that optimizes water usage based on real-time soil moisture and weather data.

AI Detection & Irrigation Capabilities
